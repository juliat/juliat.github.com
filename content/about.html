Hi, I'm Julia.

I'm currently a senior at Carnegie Mellon University. My majors and minor are a mouthful; essentially, I study how to design technology which solves problems for organizations and individuals, and which does so gracefully. I have experience managing projects, conducting user research, creating low and high fidelity prototypes, designing documents and information visualizations, and programming for the web.
